Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly do you do? Are you selling me software?

No products, no subscriptions to some platform we built. We're advisors first: we learn your business, figure out where the real opportunities are, and build or run the specific thing that addresses it. We also run the ongoing work if you want us to, since we're not just a consultant who leaves you with a report.

Why AI, and why now?

AI has changed what a small team can take on. A business with a handful of employees can now have the kind of consistency and follow-through that used to require a much bigger team. We're not here to sell AI as a concept; we're here to tell you honestly which parts of that actually apply to you.

My business is doing fine. Why would I need this?

Fair question, and often the honest answer is: you might not, not yet. If growth has come mostly from referrals and timing rather than anything deliberate, there's usually something worth exploring. If that's not you, we'll say so on the call.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Owner-operated local businesses in Sonoma County and the broader North Bay: wineries, restaurants, hospitality, retail, and other small and mid-sized operations where the owner is still closely involved in day-to-day work.

Do you just tell us what to do, or do we actually have a say?

This is built to be collaborative, not prescriptive. You know your customers and your business better than we ever will, so we work with you on what actually fits how you operate, not a one-size-fits-all package.

Will AI replace my staff?

No. Think of it as taking the repetitive, administrative weight off the people you already have, so they spend more time on the parts of the job that actually need a person.

Do I need to be technical to work with you?

Not at all. That's the point of hiring an advisor instead of buying software. We handle the technical side and explain everything in plain language.

What does it cost?

The first consultation is free, always. If there's a real fit, we typically start with a small setup fee, plus a low monthly rate to manage and monitor it, including a plain-language monthly readout. No long-term contract required to get started.
